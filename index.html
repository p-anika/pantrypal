Conquer Food Waste, One Meal at a Time.

Food waste is a global problem, and it has a significant impact on our environment and economy. Every year, a massive amount of food is thrown away, contributing to greenhouse gas emissions and wasting valuable resources. But the good news is that we all have a role to play in solving this.

Key Facts About Food Waste

30-40%

of the food supply is wasted in the United States.

$218 B

is the estimated annual cost of food waste in the US.

11%

of global greenhouse gas emissions come from food waste.

By making small changes in how we manage our food, we can make a huge difference. This website provides the tools you need to track your inventory, find recipes for what you already have, and even share extra food with your community.
